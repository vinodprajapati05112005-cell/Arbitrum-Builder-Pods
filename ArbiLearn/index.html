Why Layer 2?

Ethereum Needed a Solution

Ethereum is powerful but faces real limitations. Arbitrum solves them.

⛽

High Gas Fees

Simple transactions on Ethereum mainnet can cost $20–$100+ during peak times, making small transactions economically unviable.

🐢

Slow Throughput

Ethereum processes ~15 transactions per second. Visa handles 24,000 TPS. This bottleneck limits real-world adoption.

⚡

Arbitrum's Fix

Arbitrum processes transactions off-chain in batches, then posts proofs to Ethereum — inheriting its security at a fraction of the cost.

How Arbitrum Works

  • ✦ Transactions are processed on Arbitrum's off-chain network
  • ✦ Batches are compressed and submitted to Ethereum L1
  • ✦ Fraud proofs ensure security without trusting the sequencer
  • ✦ Users get Ethereum-level security at 10–100x lower cost
  • ✦ Fully EVM-compatible — existing Solidity contracts work as-is
